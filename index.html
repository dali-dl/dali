About Me

Da Li is a Researcher within the Machine Learning Group in Samsung AI Centre Cambridge, and also a member of the Machine Intelligence Research group at the University of Edinburgh as a Postdoctoral Researcher.

His research interests span transfer learning, meta learning, semi-supervised learning and automatic speech recognition.
